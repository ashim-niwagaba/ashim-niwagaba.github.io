Wrox Recipes-the world's best scrambled eggs

Recipe

I got about this recipe from the internet on Artisan Backery website.
This ingredient is what everybody needs if they are
to have breakfast, i meant real breakfast not just breakfast

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of butter(10g)
  • 1/3 cup of cream(2 3/4 fl ounces)
  • A pinch of salt
  • Freshy milled black pepper
  • 3 fresh chieves(chopped)

Instructions

  1. Whisk eggs, cream and salt in a bowl
  2. Melt the butterin a none sticky pan over a high heat(taking care not to burn the butter)
  3. Pour egg mixture in the pan and wait untill its starts setting around theedge of the pan(20seconds)
  4. Using a woden spatula, bring the mixture into the center as it were omlet and let it cook for another 20seconds
  5. Fold contents in, leave for 20 seconds, and repeat untill the eggs are just only done
  6. Grind a light sprinkling freshy milled pepper over the eggs and blend in some chopped fresh chives
